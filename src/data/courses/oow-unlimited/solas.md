# SOLAS — OOW Exam Focus

SOLAS is the primary international safety convention covering construction, fire protection, LSA, navigation equipment, and security. Examiners expect you to know which chapter covers what and the key requirements within each.

KEY EXAM AREAS:
- Chapter II-1 (construction and subdivision) and II-2 (fire protection — A-class/B-class divisions, fire zones)
- Chapter III (life-saving appliances — requirements by vessel type and area of operation)
- Chapter V (safety of navigation — equipment carriage, ECDIS mandate, voyage planning obligations)
- Chapter IX (ISM Code — SMS elements, DPA role, DOC and SMC certificates)
- Chapter XI-2 (ISPS Code — three security levels, SSP, SSO, CSO responsibilities)

COMMON MISTAKES:
- Confusing A-class and B-class fire division ratings and their time/temperature requirements
- Not knowing ECDIS carriage is now mandatory (phase-in complete) per Chapter V
- Mixing up ISM certificates — DOC (company) vs SMC (ship)
- Not knowing PSC detention authority and what triggers an inspection
- Forgetting that SOLAS V/34 means Master's authority on safety cannot be overridden
